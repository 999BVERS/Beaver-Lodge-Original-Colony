Chapter I Recap: Kicking Off the BLOC Competition Series

Every great journey begins with a first step. A look back at Chapter I: Welcome to BLOC — the holder-exclusive opening chapter of Competition Series Season 1.

BLOC Team
· July 18, 2026 · 3 min read

Every great journey begins with a first step.

Chapter I: Welcome to BLOC officially kicked off the BLOC Competition Series Season 1, marking the start of a creative journey that will continue throughout the season. As the inaugural chapter, it served as a special opening event dedicated exclusively to BLOC holders — recognizing the early supporters who helped build the foundation of the community.

Participants were challenged to create a poster that captured what BLOC meant to them. The result was a diverse collection of creative interpretations, each reflecting a unique perspective on the project and its values.

Chapter I Champion

After careful evaluation by our panel of judges, Viper(@ViperrSolGodNFT) was named the Chapter I Champion.

Viper's entry stood out for its creativity, storytelling, and strong representation of the chapter's theme, earning the highest overall score.

As the Chapter I Champion, Viper received the 0.2 SOL Chapter Prize and, more importantly, earned a place in the BLOC Competition Series Season 1 Finals.

There, Viper will compete alongside the champions from the remaining chapters for the title of Season 1 Champion and the season's grand prizes, including the exclusive customized 1-of-1 BLOC Beaver.

The Journey Continues

While Chapter I was created as a holders-exclusive kickoff, the Competition Series continues to grow.

Future chapters and seasons will introduce new opportunities, welcoming even more builders to showcase their talents, compete, and become part of the BLOC story.

Chapter I will always be remembered as the beginning — the chapter where BLOC holders set the standard, inspired the community, and launched a competition series built on creativity, collaboration, and the spirit of building together. Beavers will live in a saying "Strong Alone, Unstopabble Together.", forever.
