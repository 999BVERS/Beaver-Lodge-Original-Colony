BLOC's First Community Night — What We Built Together

More than 60 members showed up for our inaugural night. Here's what happened, what we learned, and what's coming next.

AL
Alex Lim
· June 15, 2025 · 4 min read

Last Friday, the Colony came alive. Over 60 members gathered for our very first Community Night — an evening of introductions, ideas, and the kind of energy that reminds you why you started building in the first place.

What happened

We opened the night with a quick overview of what BLOC is and where we're headed. Then we let the room take over. Members introduced themselves, shared what they're working on, and connected with people they'd only known online until that night.

"I came not knowing anyone. I left with three potential collaborators and a business card I didn't have before." — Colony Member

Better Business Beaver also got its first live showcase. Six featured businesses presented their work to the room, and the response was incredible. People were pulling out phones, following accounts, and making introductions on the spot.

What we learned

  • People want more face time — virtual is great but in-person hits different
  • The BBB feature is our strongest hook for new members
  • We need a bigger venue next time

What's next

Community Night is now a monthly event. The next one is scheduled for July — details coming soon. If you want to present your business or brand at the next BBB showcase, applications are open now.

Thank you to everyone who showed up, shared their story, and made the Colony feel real. This is just the beginning.
