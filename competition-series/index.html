BLOC Competition Series: A Platform for Builders and Creators

A seasonal platform for builders and creators of every kind — open to holders and non-holders alike — built to celebrate creativity, innovation, and the diverse skills people bring to the Colony.

BLOC Team
· July 2026 · 5 min read

Communities are built by people who are willing to create, contribute, and push ideas forward. The BLOC Competition Series was created to give those builders a place to showcase their talents, challenge themselves, and grow alongside the BLOC community.

More than a competition, it is an ongoing platform designed to celebrate creativity, innovation, and the diverse skills that people bring to the table. Whether you're a BLOC holder or someone discovering the community for the first time, the Competition Series is open to anyone ready to build.

What is the BLOC Competition Series?

The BLOC Competition Series is a seasonal competition where each season focuses on a specific creative discipline. Rather than testing every skill at once, each season is dedicated to a single field, allowing participants to compete fairly while exploring different challenges within that discipline.

One season may focus on poster design. Another could explore photography, writing, animation, video editing, music, 3D art, game development, coding, or entirely new forms of creativity. Every season introduces fresh opportunities while staying true to the same goal: encouraging people to build through their talents.

A Competition for Everyone

The Competition Series welcomes both holders and non-holders.

We believe that creativity shouldn't be limited by ownership. By opening the competition to everyone, BLOC creates a space where artists, designers, developers, storytellers, musicians, creators, and innovators can share their work, gain recognition, and become part of a growing creative community.

Everyone starts with the same opportunity — to create something meaningful.

More Than Winning

While every season crowns champions, the Competition Series is about more than earning a title.

It is about developing skills, exploring new ideas, inspiring others, and contributing to a community that values creation over consumption. Every entry adds something unique to BLOC, helping shape its culture and encouraging others to participate.

Whether someone wins or not, every participant becomes part of the story.

Building the Future

The BLOC Competition Series reflects the philosophy behind the beaver — the builder that creates something lasting through persistence, collaboration, and purpose.

Every season introduces new challenges. Every chapter inspires new ideas. Every participant helps build the next chapter of BLOC.

Because building isn't limited to one skill. It's a mindset.
