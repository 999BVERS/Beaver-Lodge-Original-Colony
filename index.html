About the Colony

More than a community

Beaver Lodge Original Colony (BLOC) was born from a simple idea: that people do better when they build together. We are a collective of doers, creators, and community builders united by a common drive to grow.

Our Colony exists to give members real tools, real visibility, and real connections. Whether you're launching a business, building a personal brand, or just looking for your people — this is your home base.

Through programs like Better Business Beaver, community articles, and partner spotlights, we turn potential into momentum.
